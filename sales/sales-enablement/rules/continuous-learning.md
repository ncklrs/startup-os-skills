### Continuous Learning Framework

| Component | Purpose | Frequency | Owner |
|-----------|---------|-----------|-------|
| **Microlearning** | Bite-sized skill reinforcement | Daily/Weekly | Enablement |
| **Peer Learning** | Best practice sharing | Weekly | Reps + Managers |
| **Manager Coaching** | 1:1 skill development | Weekly | Managers |
| **Deep Dives** | Topic mastery | Monthly | Enablement/SMEs |
| **External Learning** | Industry knowledge | Quarterly | Individual |
| **Certifications** | Validated competency | Annual | Enablement |


### Microlearning Best Practices

| Principle | Implementation | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **Short** | 3-7 minutes max | Single concept video |
| **Focused** | One skill or idea | "How to ask budget questions" |
| **Interactive** | Quiz or activity | Scenario with choices |
| **Mobile** | Accessible anywhere | App or mobile-friendly LMS |
| **Spaced** | Regular intervals | Daily or every other day |
| **Applied** | Action to try | "Try this on your next call" |

### Measuring Learning Engagement

| Metric | Target | What It Tells You |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Completion Rate** | 85%+ | Participation level |
| **Satisfaction Score** | 4.2/5+ | Content quality |
| **Knowledge Retention** | 80%+ on follow-up | Actual learning |
| **Time Spent** | Varies | Engagement depth |
| **Return Visits** | 3+/month | Ongoing value |
| **Peer Participation** | 70%+ | Cultural adoption |

### Anti-Patterns

- **Training events only** ‚Äî No ongoing reinforcement
- **Same content for all** ‚Äî No personalization by level/role
- **Passive consumption** ‚Äî No application or practice
- **Manager non-participation** ‚Äî Leaders don't model learning
- **Learning as punishment** ‚Äî Only underperformers get "trained"
- **No time allocated** ‚Äî "Learn on your own time"
- **Content dump** ‚Äî 500 resources with no curation
- **Forgetting the forgetting curve** ‚Äî Train once, assume retention
