**Impact: HIGH**

| Stage | Content Type | Purpose | Owner |
|-------|--------------|---------|-------|
| **Awareness** | Blog posts, social content, webinars | Attract and educate | Marketing |
| **Consideration** | Solution briefs, comparison guides | Build interest | Product Marketing |
| **Evaluation** | Case studies, ROI calculators, demos | Prove value | PMM + Enablement |
| **Decision** | Proposals, security docs, references | Close deal | Sales + Legal |
| **Onboarding** | Implementation guides, training | Ensure success | Customer Success |

### Content Audit Framework

| Attribute | Score (1-5) | Action |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Relevance** | Is it still accurate? | Update or retire |
| **Usage** | Are reps using it? | Promote or retire |
| **Performance** | Does it help win deals? | Amplify or revise |
| **Findability** | Can reps find it quickly? | Improve tagging |
| **Freshness** | When was it last updated? | Schedule refresh |

### Content Effectiveness Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Benchmark |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| **Content Usage** | % of reps using content | >70% |
| **Asset Engagement** | Views, time spent, shares | Varies |
| **Influence on Win Rate** | Win % when content shared | +15-20% |
| **Time to Find** | Seconds to locate content | <30 sec |
| **Content ROI** | Revenue influenced / content cost | 10x+ |
| **Content Freshness** | % of content <12 months old | >80% |
| **Coverage Gaps** | Stages/personas without content | 0 critical |

### Content Governance

| Content Type | Approval Required | Review Cycle | Retire After |
|--------------|------------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Case Studies** | Customer + Legal | 6 months | Logo permission |
| **Pricing Docs** | RevOps | Per change | Immediately |
| **Competitive** | PMM | Monthly | When outdated |
| **Templates** | Enablement | Quarterly | When unused |
| **Legal/Security** | Legal | Annually | Never (archive) |

### Anti-Patterns

- **Content hoarding** â€” Marketing owns, sales can't access
- **No version control** â€” Multiple outdated versions floating around
- **Search dysfunction** â€” Can't find content, reps give up
- **No usage tracking** â€” Don't know what works
- **Customer approval gaps** â€” Using case studies without permission
- **One-way creation** â€” Marketing creates, never asks sales what's needed
- **Stale content** â€” Case studies from 3 years ago with departed customers
- **Feature sheets** â€” Specs without benefits or use cases
