## Certification Programs

**Impact: MEDIUM-HIGH**

Certifications validate competency and create accountability. Well-designed programs ensure sellers can actually do the job, not just pass a test.

### Why Certifications Matter

| Benefit | For the Seller | For the Organization |
|---------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Validation** | Confidence in skills | Consistent competency |
| **Accountability** | Clear expectations | Quality control |
| **Career Path** | Progression markers | Retention tool |
| **Compliance** | Access to tools/territories | Risk management |
| **Recognition** | Achievement and status | Culture of excellence |

### Certification Program Types

| Type | Purpose | Frequency | Pass Criteria |
|------|---------|-----------|---------------|
| **Role Certification** | Baseline competency for job | Once (at ramp complete) | 85%+ |
| **Product Certification** | Deep product knowledge | Per major release | 80%+ |
| **Methodology Certification** | Sales process mastery | Annual | Role-play pass |
| **Advanced Certification** | Expert-level skills | Optional, career path | 90%+ |
| **Compliance Certification** | Legal/regulatory requirements | Annual | 100% |

### Good Certification Design

```markdown
## Enterprise Account Executive Certification

### Overview
- **Duration:** 8 weeks (embedded in onboarding)
- **Components:** 4 modules + final assessment
- **Pass Criteria:** 85% overall, must pass each module

---

### Module 1: Product & Market (Week 2)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Explain core product capabilities and use cases
- Articulate value proposition by persona
- Position against top 3 competitors

**Assessment:**
| Component | Weight | Format | Pass |
|-----------|--------|--------|------|
| Knowledge Quiz | 40% | 25 questions, multiple choice | 80% |
| Competitive Scenario | 30% | Written response | Rubric |
| Elevator Pitch | 30% | Video recording, peer review | Rubric |

---

### Module 2: Discovery & Qualification (Week 4)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Execute MEDDIC qualification framework
- Conduct effective discovery conversations
- Identify and develop champions

**Assessment:**
| Component | Weight | Format | Pass |
|-----------|--------|--------|------|
| MEDDIC Quiz | 30% | Scenario-based questions | 80% |
| Discovery Role-Play | 70% | Live with manager | Rubric pass |

**Role-Play Rubric:**
| Skill | Exceeds (3) | Meets (2) | Developing (1) | Missing (0) |
|-------|-------------|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| Opening | Sets clear agenda, builds rapport | Sets agenda | Unclear opening | No agenda |
| Questions | Multi-level, insightful | Good coverage | Surface-level | Few questions |
| Listening | Probes, summarizes, confirms | Responds appropriately | Talks too much | Doesn't listen |
| MEDDIC | All elements uncovered | Most elements | Some elements | Few elements |
| Next Steps | Clear, committed, mutual | Proposed | Vague | None |

**Pass:** Score 2+ on all skills, no 0s

---

### Module 3: Demo & Presentation (Week 6)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Deliver tailored product demonstrations
- Tell compelling customer stories
- Handle technical questions confidently

**Assessment:**
| Component | Weight | Format | Pass |
|-----------|--------|--------|------|
| Demo Role-Play | 60% | Live with manager + SE | Rubric pass |
| Technical Q&A | 20% | Rapid-fire questions | 70% |
| Storytelling | 20% | Case study presentation | Rubric |

---

### Module 4: Negotiation & Close (Week 8)
**Learning Objectives:**
- Navigate pricing conversations
- Handle procurement negotiations
- Execute closing techniques

**Assessment:**
| Component | Weight | Format | Pass |
|-----------|--------|--------|------|
| Pricing Quiz | 30% | Scenario-based | 80% |
| Negotiation Role-Play | 50% | Live with manager | Rubric pass |
| Deal Review | 20% | Present live opportunity | Rubric |

---

### Final Certification
**Overall Score Calculation:**
- Module 1: 20%
- Module 2: 30%
- Module 3: 25%
- Module 4: 25%

**Requirements to Certify:**
☐ 85% overall score
☐ Pass each module (no module below 70%)
☐ Pass all role-plays (manager sign-off)
☐ Complete within 8 weeks of start date

**Consequences:**
- **Pass:** Full territory assignment, eligible for quota
- **Conditional Pass (70-84%):** Extended coaching, 30-day retest
- **Fail (<70%):** Performance review, intensive remediation

### Certification Maintenance
- Annual recertification quiz (80% pass)
- Methodology refresher role-play
- Product release certifications as required
```

### Bad Certification Design

```markdown
## Sales Certification (What NOT to Do)

### Assessment
- 50-question multiple choice quiz
- Covers everything from product to process
- Open book, no time limit
- 60% to pass
- One attempt, no retakes for 6 months

---

PROBLEMS:
✗ Tests knowledge, not skill
✗ No practical application
✗ Too easy to game
✗ No rubrics or standards
✗ Binary pass/fail with no development path
✗ No ongoing maintenance
✗ No connection to actual performance
```

### Assessment Methods Comparison

| Method | Best For | Validity | Effort | Scale |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|-------|
| **Multiple Choice** | Knowledge recall | Low | Low | High |
| **Scenario-Based Questions** | Applied knowledge | Medium | Medium | High |
| **Written Responses** | Thought process | Medium | High | Medium |
| **Role-Play (Peer)** | Skill practice | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| **Role-Play (Manager)** | Skill validation | High | High | Low |
| **Live Observation** | Real-world skill | Very High | Very High | Very Low |
| **Performance Data** | Actual results | Very High | Low | High |

### Role-Play Best Practices

**Setup:**
- Provide scenario 24 hours in advance
- Clear role-play parameters (time, scope)
- Evaluator uses standardized rubric
- Record for coaching purposes

**During:**
- Evaluator stays in character
- No interruptions except time warnings
- Take notes on rubric

**After:**
- Self-assessment first: "How did you think it went?"
- Share rubric scores with specific examples
- Identify 1-2 development areas
- Schedule follow-up practice

### Compliance Certification Requirements

| Area | Content | Frequency | Consequences |
|------|---------|-----------|--------------|
| **Legal/Ethics** | Bribery, gifts, compliance | Annual | Selling suspended |
| **Data Privacy** | GDPR, CCPA, data handling | Annual | Account access revoked |
| **Product Claims** | What you can/can't promise | Per release | Deal review required |
| **Competitive** | What you can/can't say | Quarterly | Legal review |
| **Security** | Customer data handling | Annual | System access revoked |

### Anti-Patterns

- **Knowledge-only testing** — Quizzes without skill assessment
- **Too easy to pass** — 60% threshold, unlimited attempts
- **No consequences** — Certification optional with no enforcement
- **One-and-done** — No recertification or maintenance
- **Generic rubrics** — "Good, needs improvement" without specifics
- **Manager gaming** — Passing everyone regardless of performance
- **No calibration** — Different standards across managers
- **Disconnected from performance** — Certified but can't sell
