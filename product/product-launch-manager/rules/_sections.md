**Impact:** CRITICAL
**Description:** Launch day operations, war rooms, runbooks, and real-time decision making. The moment of truth.

## 6. Post-Launch Operations (postlaunch)
**Impact:** HIGH
**Description:** Monitoring, retrospectives, metrics analysis, and iterative improvement. The launch isn't over when it ships.
